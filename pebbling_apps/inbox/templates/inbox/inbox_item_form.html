About Manual Inbox Items

This form allows you to manually add items to your inbox for testing the inbox functionality. In normal usage, items are added automatically when feeds are processed.

  • Title: A descriptive title for the item
  • Description: Optional notes or description
  • Source: Where this item came from (defaults to "manual")
